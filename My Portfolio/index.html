Interview Questions

What kind of position are you looking for?

I'm looking for a developer position that prioritizes design, user experience, and compatibility.

What are your skills?

CSS3, HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery, User Experience, Cross Browser Compatibility, UI Design, Pixel Perfect Development, Responsive Design and Development.

How do you stay on top of current design trends?

I frequent dribbble and Behance to see what my favorite designers are up to. Awwwards, CSS-Tricks, and Mozilla Developer Network are great to keep my code up to date.

What tools and programs do you use?

Brackets / Sublime Text / Atom, Photoshop, Illustrator Chrome / FireFox / IE Developer Tools, Cyberduck.

How comfortable are you with writing code entirely by hand?

Very comfortable; this is my preferred method. I like to have full control of my work and make sure I'm using the most up-to-date code. However, I am also very experienced working with WYSIWYG editors if necessary.

If you had a magic wand and could create the perfect job, what would it be?

I'd like a job that encourages creative thinking and problem solving. I'm a proactive individual with a desire to transfer my experience to a great company.
